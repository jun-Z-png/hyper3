Hyper is an interactive experience exploring neurodivergent perception of time, sound, and space through a playful,poetic, and metaphoric journey.

Each level presents a different aspects of life — sensory overload, time and self, social interactions— reflecting what makes up the world of neurodivergent and how to emphasize across the spectrum.

This project was created as part of a thesis at Parsons School of Design, MFA Design and Technology, graduating May 2026.

Designed and developed by Jun Zhou. For inquiries, visit junzhou.info.
